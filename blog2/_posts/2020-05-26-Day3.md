# The Two Cultures of Computational Psychiatry by Yael Niv

## Why I chose it

I have interests in computational psychiatry and this seems like a good introduction to a high level view of the field.

## Papers to check out 
Check out papers 1 and 2.


## Content summary

There are 2 computational psychiatry cultures.

1. The machine learning one, which uses classifiers on patient features to predict psychiatric outcome. Doesn't care for psychological or neurological explanations

2. Explanatory models. Statistical models with parameters with neurological or psychological meanings.

The paper also mentions in broad terms how to implement cognitive RL models, things that are explained in a book about the topic (fit models, simulate data)
