# Does mindfulness change the mind? A novel psychonectome perspective based on Network Analysis by Roca P. et al.

## Why I chose it

Reading for journal club about mindfulness.


## Content summary

They compare changes in time of the connectome between behavioral/questionnaire measures (psychonectome) after doing a mindfulness training intervention.

## Writing
